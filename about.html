About Me

My Journey

I've been fascinated by technology since I was small. I have a happy memory of the first time I disassembled a computer to investigate how it operated. Although I didn't completely succeed in reassembling it, the experience sparked my curiosity. This inquisitiveness has developed into a deep fascination with the ways in which technology may address practical issues.

I've committed myself to studying a variety of IT-related topics, such as cybersecurity, systems analysis, and programming. Every new finding has increased my enthusiasm and dedication to becoming an expert in this dynamic field.

What I Do

As an ICT Tutor at Boineelo Primary School, I play a crucial role in enhancing both student learning and educator support. I am currently guiding a group of learners as they prepare for the science expo focused on Eskom, helping them to explore and innovate in the field of science.

In addition to supporting learners, I have successfully assisted several educators in achieving their coursework objectives, ensuring they have the necessary tools and resources to excel. My contributions are significantly advancing the educational experience at Boineelo Primary School.
